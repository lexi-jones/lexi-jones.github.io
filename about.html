--- layout: single title: About permalink: / author_profile: true --- Hello! I am Lexi Jones-Kellett, a Biological Oceanography Ph.D. candidate in the MIT-WHOI Joint Program. I work in Mick Follows' Darwin Project group at MIT. Before that, I obtained an Honors Scholar B.A. in Mathematics with Minors in Physics and Business from Temple University. I first became interested in scientific research working in Rob Kulthinal's [Evolutionary Genetics and Biological Informatics](http://www.bioinform.org/) lab.
